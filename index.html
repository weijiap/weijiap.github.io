About Me

I am an Assistant Professor of Business Analytics and Finance at the Jack Welch College of Business and Technology, Sacred Heart University.

I received my Ph.D. in Financial Econometrics from Rutgers University–New Brunswick in 2020. Prior to that, I earned a B.A. in Economics and a B.Sc. in Mathematics from Central University of Finance and Economics, China, in 2014.

My research interests include time series analysis and forecasting, machine learning and big data in finance, ESG and climate finance, and FinTech.
