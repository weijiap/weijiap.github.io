ESG Disclosure Analytics & Rating Prediction

NLP Machine Learning

Built an end-to-end NLP pipeline over 10,000+ SEC 10-K filings from 450+ S&P 500 companies (2007–2025). Used FinBERT and SentenceTransformer to score each filing across five ESG discourse dimensions: rhetoric appearance, concrete commitment, vague talk, climate risk exposure, and financial sentiment. Engineered a novel green hype gap metric quantifying the mismatch between ESG rhetoric and substantive action. Trained an XGBoost model (strict temporal train/val/test split) to predict MSCI ESG ratings, achieving strong out-of-sample performance. Applied SHAP TreeExplainer with interaction analysis to reveal a key nonlinearity: ESG rhetoric provides legitimacy gains that diminish sharply for high-carbon-intensity firms — uncovering a systemic tension in how ESG rating agencies weigh disclosure.

Stack: Python · FinBERT · SentenceTransformer · XGBoost · SHAP · SEC EDGAR · LSEG · MSCI · WRDS / Compustat

Mixed-Frequency Macro-Financial Forecasting Engine

Machine Learning

Designed a scalable forecasting system integrating heterogeneous high- and low-frequency financial and macroeconomic data to predict asset returns, realized volatility, exchange rates, and credit ratings. Built state-space mixed-frequency factor models alongside random forest and ensemble methods, with a non-parametric intraday volatility decomposition applied to 5-minute S&P 500 data (2006–2018) and 30+ macro variables. Achieved significant out-of-sample R² improvements over benchmark models. Implemented a cross-sectional market contagion measure that boosts sector-level return predictability by capturing correlation spillovers across industry groups.

Stack: Python · R · MIDAS · State-Space Models · Random Forest · High-Frequency Financial Data

Causal Inference on ESG Governance & Global Investment

Causal Inference

Led two large-scale causal inference projects using firm-level and country-level panel data. On the firm side: applied Double Machine Learning (DML) and Instrumental Variables (IV) on a panel of 6,700+ firm-years to isolate the causal effect of ESG disclosure quality on financial outcomes, controlling for endogenous reporting incentives. On the investment side: used Dynamic Panel GMM, Panel VAR, and Granger causality tests on 189-country FDI data to estimate how biodiversity conservation and natural capital policies affect foreign direct investment flows. All specifications include extensive robustness checks (Hausman tests, over-identification, weak-instrument diagnostics).

Stack: Python · R · Double ML · IV · Dynamic Panel GMM · Panel VAR · Granger Causality
