ESG Disclosure Analytics & Rating Prediction

NLP Machine Learning
450+ S&P 500 companies · 10,000+ SEC 10-K filings · 2007–2025 · LSEG · MSCI · WRDS

Built a production-grade NLP pipeline to quantify what companies actually say about ESG versus what they do — and whether that gap predicts ESG ratings. Downloaded and parsed all S&P 500 10-K filings from SEC EDGAR; scored each sentence across five ESG discourse dimensions using FinBERT and SentenceTransformer embeddings; engineered a green hype gap metric (rhetoric minus concrete action) as a novel disclosure signal. Trained an XGBoost regressor on a strict temporal split to predict MSCI ESG ratings out-of-sample. Applied SHAP interaction analysis to surface the key finding: ESG rhetoric boosts ratings, but the effect diminishes sharply for high-carbon firms — revealing a systemic tension in how agencies score disclosure.

Python FinBERT SentenceTransformer XGBoost SHAP SEC EDGAR LSEG MSCI Compustat

Mixed-Frequency Macro-Financial Forecasting

Machine Learning
5-min intraday S&P 500 data · 30+ macro variables · 2006–2025

Engineered a scalable forecasting system that blends high-frequency intraday price data with low-frequency macroeconomic indicators to predict asset returns, realized volatility, exchange rates, and credit ratings. Built non-parametric intraday volatility decompositions, state-space mixed-frequency factor models, and a cross-sectional market contagion measure that captures correlation spillovers across industry groups. Causal Inference on ESG Governance & Global Investment

Causal Inference
6,700+ firm-years · 189 countries · FDI · ESG · Biodiversity

Applied modern causal ML to two large-scale policy questions. Firm-level: used Double Machine Learning (DML) and Instrumental Variables on a 6,700+ firm-year panel to isolate the causal effect of ESG disclosure quality on financial outcomes, controlling for endogenous reporting incentives and firm-level confounders. Country-level: applied Dynamic Panel GMM, Panel VAR, and Granger causality on 189-country FDI data to estimate how biodiversity conservation policies affect cross-border investment flows. All specifications include Hausman tests, over-identification tests, and weak-instrument diagnostics.

Python R Double ML Instrumental Variables Dynamic Panel GMM Panel VAR Granger Causality
